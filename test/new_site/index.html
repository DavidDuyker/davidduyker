Past Lives

Before working in digital tech my passion for the circular economy and clothing repair lead me to Boulder, Colorado where I was on the leadership team at an experimental Pop-Up Patagonia store. We sold exclusively pre-owned clothing and repaired gear from any brand on the spot.

I also had a stint designing outdoor gear in the Cascade Mountains. During my time working in a small studio I wielded a pattern knife as I cut and sewed prototypes, as well as breathed new life into their brand through a new handmade website.
