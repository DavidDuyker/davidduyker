Refresh

Work stress is reported to be a major factor in health and quality of life. How might an intelligent, personalized system encourage better workplace health?

Many of us will end up spending a large portion of our lives in one workplace or the other. The demands from work often lead workers to neglect their mental and physical well being to the detriment of health and productivity.

Refresh is an office tool set that nudges workers to practice better habits based on their own stress levels and preferences. Through galvanic skin response (GSR) the mouse and keyboard are able to accurately measure stress levels and provide a path to calm.
